About Us

The I-Kid humanoid soccer robot team is supported by the School of Instrumentation Science and Opto-Electronic Engineering. The team actively explores humanoid robot structural design, machine vision, gait planning, and behavior decision-making, continuously upgrading both hardware platforms and theoretical algorithms. Since its establishment in 2010, the team has recruited graduate and undergraduate students every year, cultivating a large number of professional talents and significantly enhancing students’ practical and innovative abilities. I-Kid is an important platform for outstanding talent development at our university. The team has achieved remarkable competition results, including winning the championship at the 2024 RoboCup Asia-Pacific Tianjin International Invitational and multiple awards in the RoboCup China from 2021 to 2025. Members also participated in RoboCup international competitions in the humanoid small group in the Netherlands, Brazil, Japan, Canada, and Australia, learning from and exchanging ideas with international peers, which improved their international communication skills.
